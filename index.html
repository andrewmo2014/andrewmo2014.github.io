VR Film

 A dimly lit train cabin … swaying grassy plains … flickering fireflies … a mighty dragon. 

Found is an interactive short film experience for virtual reality built in Unity3D. It's story speaks to the connection and lack there of between nature, technology and fellow travelers as you make your way through a fantastical yet familiar world. With an awe eerily reminiscent of a Miyazaki film, this departure from traditional gameplay delivers the feels we’ve been dreaming of since VR landed.

We developed this experience in less than a month as part of the UploadVR + MakeSchool VR Academy to showcase our talents and vision of creating a VR cinematic experience that accommodates limited user interaction. The narrative, art, and gameplay mechanics are a proof of concept we plan to improve on later iterations or carry over to future content. Our application has been featured by Oculus Launch Pad and received Best Motion Picture: Fantasy & Sci-Fi VR Award at Cinequest Film & VR Festival 2017.
